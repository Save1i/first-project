Itero 5D-Scanner & NIRI - How to Use It?

NIRI stands for Near Infra-Red Imaging, which is a system used for early detection of enamel lesions. It offers higher sensitivity compared to bitewing radiography and has already demonstrated comparable sensitivity in detecting dentin lesions.

A multicenter clinical study has confirmed the benefits of this system as a tool for detecting and monitoring interproximal caries lesions above the gingiva without the need for X-ray radiation.

Journal of Dentistry (As of October 24, 2021)
